About Us
Farmer's Table is a startup. We specialize in Natural to organic farming and producing naturally made food products like home-made pickles, natural regional masalas, organic honey, and more. We cater to clients who are health and environmentally conscious and want to expand by providing to more clients who may or may not be well versed with organic foods and sustainable living.
