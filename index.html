About Us

Farmer’s Table came about due to the realization that the food on the table was not always of good quality and original traditional flavors. Hence, Farmer’s Table was born. We realized that along with farmer's table owned farms there are a lot of other small & marginal farmers who practice natural and organic farming.

Farmer’s Table ensures that these quality products from our farms and other such small farmers, reaches your table curated and checked for quality, texture and flavor.
So, you always get quality ingredients and agro - products.

We are also ensuring revival of certain traditional home made recipes and products like pickles, sun dried chilly, etc, which are not easily available in quality, so that these niche traditional flavors can reach your table and your palate.
